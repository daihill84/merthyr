Our Humane Welfare & Legal Policy

At Welsh Town & Country, we follow the Wildlife and Countryside Act 1981 to the letter. It is a criminal offence to release a trapped grey squirrel back into the wild.

Why we use Humane Kill Traps: We prioritize animal welfare. Live-trapping causes extreme stress during transport and subsequent dispatch. Instead, David uses approved professional humane kill-traps housed in safety tunnels. This ensures an instantaneous end with zero stress, providing the most ethical solution for homeowners.
