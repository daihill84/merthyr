Grey squirrels are a significant threat to Merthyr lofts, particularly in houses backing onto woodland corridors. Unlike rats, squirrels have incredibly powerful teeth capable of gnawing through structural timbers, plastic water pipes, and lead flashing in hours.

Our Humane Welfare & Legal Policy

At Welsh Town & Country, we follow the Wildlife and Countryside Act 1981 to the letter. It is a criminal offence to release a trapped grey squirrel back into the wild.

Why we use Humane Kill Traps: We do not utilize live-capture traps for squirrels. Live-trapping is extremely stressful for the animal during transport and subsequent dispatch. Instead, David uses approved professional humane kill-traps housed in safety tunnels. This method ensures an instantaneous end, zero stress, and full legal compliance, providing the most ethical solution possible for both the homeowner and the animal.
