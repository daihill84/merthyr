Signs of a Rat Infestation

           

It's important to recognize the early warning signs of a rat problem:

           
                   
  • **Droppings** found near food sources, in lofts, or along pathways (usually dark, tapered pellets).
    •                
  • **Chewed wires** or damage to furniture, skirting boards, or pipework.
    •                
  • **Nests** made of shredded paper, cardboard, or fabric, often found in warm, hidden areas.
    •                
  • **Unusual noises** (scuttling, gnawing, squeaking) coming from walls, suspended ceilings, or attics, especially at night.
    •                
  • **Smear Marks** (greasy streaks) left by rats rubbing their bodies along walls and runways.
    •            
           

If you notice any of these signs, it's crucial to take immediate action to prevent further infestation and structural damage.
