Common Rat Problems in Dowlais & Gurnos

Rats are a persistent issue across upper Merthyr valleys, particularly in Dowlais and the Gurnos estate. Older housing stock, shared drainage systems and historic sewer layouts provide ideal access routes for rodents. In Dowlais, Victorian terraces and converted properties often suffer from cracked clay pipes, while parts of Gurnos experience problems linked to shared laterals and surface water drains.

Many residents first notice scratching noises, droppings in kitchens or utility rooms, or damage to waste pipes and insulation. Unfortunately, store-bought poison rarely solves the issue. In many cases it makes matters worse, leading to rats dying under floors or inside walls, creating odour problems and attracting insects.
