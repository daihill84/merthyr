The Risks of Mole Activity

Moles are more than just a nuisance. In agricultural settings, molehills contaminate silage with Listeria bacteria, which can cause serious illness in sheep and cattle. In equestrian paddocks, tunnels can collapse under the weight of a horse, leading to expensive vet bills or rider injury. In domestic gardens, they uproot expensive landscaping and create significant trip hazards.
