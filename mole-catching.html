Why Traditional Trapping Works Best

In the varied terrain of the South Wales Valleys, gassing often proves ineffective as the gas escapes through the porous shale and clay. Traditional trapping is the "gold standard." It allows us to confirm the catch and ensures that no toxins are left in the ecosystem to harm local Red Kites or family pets.
