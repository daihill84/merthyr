Moths & Carpet Beetles

Known as textile pests, the larvae of these insects feed on natural fibers like wool, silk, and leather. David uses professional residual treatments to stop the lifecycle and protect your expensive furnishings.

Silverfish Control

Thriving in damp Merthyr bathrooms and kitchens, silverfish consume the starch in wallpaper paste, book bindings, and photos. We identify the moisture source and apply targeted treatments.

Biscuit Beetles

Often found in kitchens (pantry pests), these small brown beetles infest dried food, flour, and spices. David provides deep-clean advice and professional elimination for homes and businesses.
