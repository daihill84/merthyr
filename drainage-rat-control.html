The "Sewer-to-Loft" Pathway Explained

In South Wales, especially in older areas like Dowlais, Georgetown, and Aberfan, drainage systems are often 100+ years old. Rats are world-class climbers; they use vertical soil pipes like ladders. Once they find a crack in a pipe or a missing seal, they enter the cavity wall and travel straight to the loft.

1. Forensic Detection

My HD CCTV survey pinpoints the exact breach. No guesswork. I provide you with the footage and a technical report of the findings.

2. Mechanical Blocking

I install a high-grade 316 stainless steel non-return valve. This allows waste out but prevents rats from entering. No poisons, no return visits.
