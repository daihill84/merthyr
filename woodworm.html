Signs of Woodworm Infestation

Small Round Holes
Freshly bored exit holes in timber (approx 1mm-2mm) are a sign of active infestation.
Bore Dust (Frass)
Finding fine, powdery dust underneath or around holes indicates current activity.
Weakened Timbers
Floorboards or joists that feel "spongy" or crumble at the edges.
Visible Beetles
Seeing dead or live beetles (Common Furniture Beetle) near windowsills or wood.
