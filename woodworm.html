Is Your Woodworm Infestation Active?

Fresh Exit Holes
Look for small, clean-cut holes in timber with "bright" wood inside.
Active Frass (Dust)
Fine, lemon-shaped dust underneath holes is a sign of active larvae.
Structural Damage
Spongy floorboards or crumbling joist edges need urgent professional attention.
