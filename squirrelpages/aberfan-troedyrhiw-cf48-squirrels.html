The Dangers of Grey Squirrels in CF48

Properties in Aberfan, Merthyr Vale, and Troedyrhiw are often targeted by grey squirrels due to the nearby woodland corridors. Unlike rats, squirrels have incredibly powerful teeth that can easily strip the plastic coating off 240V electrical wiring, leading to house fires.

They also destroy thermal insulation to build "dreys" (nests), reducing your home's energy efficiency and leaving behind hazardous droppings that attract flies and parasites.

Our 3-Step Removal Process

  1. Technical Survey: Identifying nesting patterns, usually in the eaves or near chimney stacks.
  2. Legal Trapping: Using safety-shrouded kill-traps for instantaneous dispatch. *Note: Releasing a trapped grey squirrel is a criminal offence.*
  3. Permanent Exclusion: Sealing the building using galvanized steel mesh that squirrels cannot gnaw through.
High-risk areas include Moy Road, Chapel Street, and Bridge Street. Any property bordering the Taff Trail or mature oak trees is a primary target.
Squirrels are diurnal; you will hear heavy "thumping" sounds during the day. Rats are nocturnal and produce lighter scratching at night.
