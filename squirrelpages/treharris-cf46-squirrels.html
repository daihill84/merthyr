Why Squirrels Target Treharris Properties

The dense woodland surrounding Parc Taf Bargoed and the Taff Trail provides the perfect habitat for grey squirrels. During the breeding and winter seasons, these rodents view your loft as a warm, safe nesting site. They can enter through gaps as small as a golf ball by gnawing through plastic soffits or wooden fascia boards.

The Hidden Risks to Your Home:

  • Electrical Hazards: Squirrels strip the insulation from electrical cabling, which is a leading cause of attic fires.
  • Insulation Damage: They soil thermal insulation with urine and droppings, reducing energy efficiency and creating smells.
  • Nocturnal Disturbance: Hear heavy "thumping" at dawn? That is almost certainly a squirrel activity.

Our 100% Legal & Humane Approach

Under the Invasive Alien Species Order, it is illegal to release a captured grey squirrel back into the wild. To uphold animal welfare, I use professional kill-traps that ensure an instantaneous and stress-free dispatch.

Permanent Proofing: Once the resident squirrels are removed, I seal entry points with galvanized steel mesh to ensure no future infestations can occur.
