Our Subscription Packages

Basic Package - £80/month

The Basic Package includes one scheduled pest control visit per month, covering essential pest management for common infestations. This plan provides:

  • Monthly routine inspections to prevent potential pest problems
  • All necessary bait and equipment supplied at no additional cost
  • Reduced rates for additional visits as needed

This plan is ideal for residential properties and smaller businesses seeking regular protection against pests without needing emergency coverage.

Upgraded Package - £120/month

Our Upgraded Package is perfect for clients needing comprehensive pest control, including both routine visits and urgent response support. This package offers:

  • Monthly routine pest control inspections and treatments
  • Emergency call-outs for immediate pest issues, available 24/7
  • Covers a broader range of pests, including rodents, insects, and wildlife
  • Follow-up visits to ensure lasting results and peace of mind

This package is ideal for larger properties, businesses, or individuals who want full coverage and fast response to any unexpected pest situations.
