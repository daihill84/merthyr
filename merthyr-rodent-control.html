Rodent Control Services: Merthyr Tydfil, Rhymney & Ebbw Vale

           

Welcome to our Rodent Control Services page. We specialize in effective solutions for rat and mouse infestations across the Heads of the Valleys, including **Merthyr Tydfil**, **Rhymney**, **Ebbw Vale**, and surrounding areas. Our team of trained professionals is dedicated to ensuring your property remains pest-free.

       

Rodents such as rats and mice are not only a nuisance but also pose serious health risks. They can spread harmful diseases like salmonella, leptospirosis, and hantavirus, which can be transmitted through their droppings, urine, and saliva. They are also notorious for causing damage by gnawing on wires, insulation, and structural materials, potentially leading to electrical fires and other hazards. Addressing a rodent infestation promptly is essential for safeguarding both your property and the health of its occupants.

       

Our rodent control services include:

   
           
  • Inspection: Thorough assessment of your property to identify signs of rodent activity such as droppings, gnaw marks, and nests.
    •        
  • Exclusion: Implementing preventive measures by sealing entry points to keep rodents out of your property.
    •        
  • Treatment: Safe and effective methods for eliminating rats and mice using traps, bait systems, or eco-friendly rodenticides.
    •        
  • Monitoring: Regular follow-ups and ongoing monitoring to ensure long-term rodent control and to prevent reinfestation.
    •    
   

Once we’ve identified the severity of the problem, our exclusion techniques help ensure that rodents can’t re-enter. Whether it’s sealing small cracks, gaps, or doorways, we cover all vulnerabilities. Our treatment options are carefully chosen to be effective yet safe for your family and pets, with rodenticides only used as a last resort and in a manner that minimizes exposure risks. Finally, our monitoring program involves routine check-ups to maintain the integrity of the treatment and prevent future issues.

Common Signs of Rodent Infestation

           
                   
  • Droppings around food packages, in drawers, or in cupboards.
    •                
  • Chewed holes in walls, floors, or furniture.
    •                
  • Nesting materials such as shredded paper or fabric.
    •                
  • Unusual noises coming from walls or attics, especially at night.
    •            
           

If you notice any of these signs, it's essential to act quickly to prevent further infestation.
